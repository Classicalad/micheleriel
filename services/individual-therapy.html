Individual Therapy

Strengthen Coping, Confidence, and Connection

Home› Services› Individual Therapy

One-on-One Support Tailored to You

Individual therapy offers a supportive, confidential space to explore what's affecting your mental health and well-being. Whether you're experiencing anxiety, low mood, overwhelm, self-esteem concerns, or the aftermath of past experiences, we'll work together to understand what's driving these experiences and build practical skills for lasting change.

My approach is person-centred and collaborative — meaning you set the pace and direction, and I provide the tools, insights, and support to help you get there. I integrate a range of evidence-based approaches tailored to your unique needs.

My Approach to Individual Therapy

My approach integrates Internal Family Systems (IFS) and Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) to help you explore how stress shows up in your body and your relationships. I also use EFT-informed approaches to support you in developing secure attachment and healthy boundaries.

We'll explore:

  • The patterns, roles, and beliefs that keep you stuck
  • How early experiences shaped your emotional responses
  • How stress and anxiety show up in your body
  • Coping strategies and regulation skills for daily life
  • Building healthier relationship patterns and boundaries
  • Reconnecting with your sense of self and self-worth

Areas I Support in Individual Therapy

  • Anxiety and worry
  • Low mood and depression
  • Stress and burnout
  • Trauma and PTSD
  • Grief and loss
  • Life transitions
  • Self-esteem and confidence
  • Neurodiversity and ADHD
  • Relationship challenges
  • Unhelpful thinking patterns
