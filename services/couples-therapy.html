Couples and Marriage Counselling

Relationships are complex — and even the strongest partnerships can hit points of disconnection, conflict, or crisis. Couples therapy offers a structured, supportive space to slow down, understand each other more deeply, and build new ways of relating.

My primary approach to couples therapy is Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT). Together we'll identify the cycle that keeps you stuck, slow things down, and create safer ways of communicating — especially when conflict, distance, or repeated misunderstandings take over.

What Couples Therapy Can Help With

  • Communication breakdowns and recurring conflict
  • Emotional distance and disconnection
  • Infidelity and rebuilding trust after betrayal
  • Parenting disagreements and family stress
  • Life transitions as a couple (new baby, career changes, loss)
  • Intimacy concerns
  • High reactivity and escalating arguments
  • Anxiety or trauma affecting the relationship
  • Navigating neurodiverse relationships

My Approach

Whether you're recovering from infidelity, managing high reactivity, or feeling emotionally disconnected, couples therapy can help. I integrate CBT strategies and IFS-informed work alongside nervous system regulation support for couples where trauma or anxiety is part of the picture.

I work with couples of all genders, orientations, and relationship structures. You don't have to be in crisis to benefit — therapy can also be a place to strengthen an already good relationship or navigate a difficult transition together.

Online Couples Therapy in Ontario and Beyond. I provide secure online therapy for couples across Ontario. While based in the Greater Toronto Area, I am also licensed to work with clients in Alberta, Saskatchewan, Manitoba, and Newfoundland.
