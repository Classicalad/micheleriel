What Is Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT) and How Can It Help Your Relationship?

Emotionally Focused Therapy is one of the most well-researched approaches for couples — and for good reason. EFT helps partners slow down the negative cycle that keeps them stuck in conflict or distance, and builds a more secure emotional connection. In this post, I explore what EFT is, how it works in practice, and what couples can expect when they begin this kind of therapy.

Anxiety is more than just worry. It's a full-body experience that can affect how we sleep, relate to others, make decisions, and move through the world. Understanding the nervous system's role in anxiety — and why it's not a character flaw — can be the first step toward meaningful change.
